# Customer Campaign Performance Analytics Platform

![Executive Overview](Dashboard_Screenshots/01_Executive_Overview.png)

## SQL • Power BI • Python • Excel • MySQL • Tableau

An end-to-end Customer Campaign Performance Analytics Platform designed to evaluate customer engagement, campaign effectiveness, conversion performance, and revenue contribution across multiple marketing channels. This project simulates a real-world banking and financial services analytics environment, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, optimize campaign strategies, and drive data-driven decision-making through interactive dashboards and business intelligence reporting.

---

# Executive Summary

Organizations invest significant resources in customer acquisition and engagement campaigns across Email, SMS, Mobile Applications, and Web platforms. However, identifying which campaigns, customer segments, and channels drive the highest business value remains a challenge.

This project provides a comprehensive analytics solution that combines Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI to transform campaign data into actionable business insights, executive reporting, and performance optimization recommendations.

# Quantified Analytics Impact

## Campaign Analytics

- Analyzed **100,000 customer-campaign interactions** to evaluate engagement, conversion behavior, and campaign effectiveness.
- Assessed performance across **50 unique marketing campaigns** spanning Credit Card, Personal Loan, Home Loan, Savings Account, and Insurance offerings.
- Evaluated **₹71.46M+ revenue contribution** generated through customer acquisition and engagement initiatives.
- Measured a **59.95% Open Rate**, demonstrating strong campaign reach and customer visibility.
- Achieved a **50.16% Click Through Rate (CTR)**, highlighting effective customer engagement.
- Identified **8,954 successful customer conversions**, supporting campaign optimization and performance monitoring.

### Customer Analytics

- Segmented **100,000 customers** using demographic, behavioral, and financial attributes.
- Evaluated customer engagement across **4 geographic regions** and **4 marketing channels**.
- Assessed customer value contribution using income, tenure, product ownership, and revenue metrics.
- Analyzed digital versus non-digital customer engagement patterns.

### Channel Analytics

- Compared performance across Email, SMS, Mobile App, and Web channels.
- Measured channel-level conversion effectiveness and revenue contribution.
- Identified high-performing communication channels supporting customer acquisition strategies.
- Evaluated customer engagement trends to improve campaign targeting.
